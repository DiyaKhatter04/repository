My Projects

Robotic Car

A robotic car project typically involves designing and building a small autonomous or remotely controlled vehicle equipped with various sensors, motors, and a microcontroller or single-board computer.The robotic car project aims to create a self-navigating or remotely operated vehicle capable of performing tasks such as obstacle avoidance, path following, and object detection. The car is powered by an onboard battery and controlled by a microcontroller like an Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or other similar devices.
